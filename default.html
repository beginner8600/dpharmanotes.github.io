Diploma in Pharmacy (D-Pharma)

01

Neelsaroj Institute for Pharmacy is one of the best D pharm colleges in Bangalore, producing cutting-edge pharmaceutical practitioners. The Pharmacy Council of India has authorized our institution, and we are associated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences. Our college has the most sophisticated infrastructures while our faculty includes the leading pharm experts in the industry.

Here, the students study the chemistry of medicines, their origins, drug development methods, drug preparation, distribution, effects, and final application for disease prevention and therapy. Our vision is to be India's foremost academic Centre for an inventive multidisciplinary research institution of pharmacy education, patient care, and community service.

Bachelor in Pharmacy (B-Pharma)

02

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B Pharmacy) is a four-year undergraduate degree programme that covers all aspects of the pharmaceutical sector, from manufacturing medications and drugs to dispensing them through a variety of medical stores, distributors, and stockists. B Pharmacy Syllabus includes Biochemical sciences and their impact on the healthcare business.

The degree allows students to gain a complete understanding of the subject's insights and essential component structure. Biochemistry, Human Anatomy and Physiology, Computer Applications in Pharmacy, and other important courses are covered in the B Pharm programme. If you are willing to take admission at NIP for pursuing B Pharm, then you have to pass an entrance examination.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)



Bangalore is one of the best places in India to pursue High-quality Pharmacy learning from top pharmacy colleges in Bangalore like Neelsaroj Institute Of Pharmacy. Bangalore is the abode of the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) and other reputed medicine and pharmaceutical institutions with huge career scope for aspiring candidates.

If you are looking to take admission to the best pharmacy colleges in Bangalore, then you can choose one of these prominent courses- B.Pharm. (Bachelor of Pharmacy), B.Pharm. (Hons.) (Bachelor of Pharmacy in Honors), B.Pharm. in Pharmaceutical Chemistry (Bachelor of Pharmacy in Pharmaceutical Chemistry), B.Pharm. in Pharmaceutics (Bachelor of Pharmacy in Pharmaceutics) etc.

Students must have completed class 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology in order to be admitted to Pharmacy programmes in top pharmacy colleges in Bangalore. Students must get a minimum of 50% on the qualifying exams. Students who have completed a three-year Pharmacy Diploma (D. Pharm) are also qualified.

After finishing your 12th grade in science, you can pursue a diploma in pharmacy if you are looking for short-term courses in pharmacy. A diploma in pharmacy is possible to obtain both before and after graduation. Pharmacy diploma (D.Pharma) that is offered by reputed pharmacy colleges in Bangalore.

The Diploma in Pharmacy (abbreviated as D Pharm or D Pharma) is a professional pharmacy certificate. It is earned after a two-year training programme. A diploma holder can work as a registered pharmacist administering medications and pharmaceuticals in a clinic or drugstore. Note: Applicants with passing marks in 10+2 are eligible for admission (lateral entry) at top pharmacy colleges in Bangalore.

Despite the fact that pharmacists must have a doctorate in pharmacy, they are neither medical doctors nor physicians.

NEET is mostly used in India for MBBS and BDS admissions. No, it is only required when applying for admission to a medical institution to pursue medical degrees such as bhms or mobs. It is not required for a diploma in pharmacy, however, you have to pass Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test to get admission to pharmacy colleges in Bangalore.

It is regarded as a doctoral degree. Yes, pharmacy is a wonderful career because a doctor provides life to the patient, but a pharmacist gives life to medication and medicine. Completing pharmacy from top pharmacy colleges in Bangalore opens the door towards bright career opportunities.
